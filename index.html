About WSA

The World Spleef Association (WSA) will be an association that is everything about spleef! As of now we are starting up a website and are looking to make things official. The website will include statistics from the tournaments, possibly interviews with some of the best, and so much more! The statistics will have stuff like who has the most wins, best W/L, etc. The WSA will have weekly tourneys that include prizes and probably other special events coming soon. We hope to add and improve for better experiences for the World Spleef Association, although this all depends on you guys so please try to help us along the way!
